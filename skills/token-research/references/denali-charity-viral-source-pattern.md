Session: 2026-05-05 — `$DENALI` / The Denali Puppies on Solana (`AVoyHUFwCDj3JACP8WZjnhK4fw8pKugHTTXhEHH3pump`).

## Pattern

A token launches around a wholesome official/public-interest source post and adds a charity/donation wrapper. In this case:

- Pump.fun metadata linked a launch tweet by `@prburman` quoting the verified U.S. Department of the Interior `@Interior` Denali sled-dog puppy-cam post.
- The official source post was real and wholesome: Denali puppy cam / new sled-dog puppies, with an official NPS page.
- Pump metadata had `is_charity=true` and framed support for Alaska Geographic.
- Early chart flow was exceptional: main PumpSwap pool around `$610k` volume in <1h, roughly `4.9k buys / 4.7k sells`, and ~$38k main LP; RugCheck/scanner risk looked good.
- Project account `@denalicoin` was brand-new/tiny (~5–6 followers, 4 tweets). TwitterAPI Latest/Top/CA search was blocked by 402 and `xurl` was unavailable.

## Scoring lesson

Classify as **WATCH**, not APE/BUY, unless there is independent official/team confirmation or overwhelming CA-specific organic proof.

Why WATCH:

- Wholesome, highly shareable narrative.
- Real official source tweet / real NPS page.
- Charity framing can amplify attention.
- Early PumpSwap flow/liquidity were unusually strong for a fresh meme.

Why not APE/BUY:

- The official DOI/NPS/charity source did **not** mention the token or CA.
- Launch tweet traction was modest (~3.8k views / 26 likes at snapshot).
- Token account was brand-new/tiny.
- Charity claim was unverified; no proof of proceeds actually reaching Alaska Geographic/NPS.
- Pump replies were 0 and X search was unavailable, so broader organic CA-specific proof was not verified.
- Copycat/charity-rug risk remains high even with a good scanner score.

## DM/watchlist phrasing

Call it a `pure meme/charity narrative` or `viral-source charity meme`, not a real product/team. Include: official source is real, but no official endorsement/CA mention; charity claim unverified; TwitterAPI search blocked if applicable.
