## Pattern

A token can justify **APE/BUY** even when the project-owned X account is tiny and TwitterAPI search is 402-blocked, if there is strong independent product evidence plus builder-owned CA confirmation.

DTOUR evidence that moved it from WATCH to APE:

- **Real downloadable/buildable product:** `detour.ninja` described a macOS menu-bar app wrapping an elizaOS AgentRuntime.
- **Public GitHub repo:** `github.com/Dexploarer/detour` had a concrete README, installer, packages, TypeScript code, releases/install flow, recent push activity, and implementation details: local `llama.cpp` embeddings, channel gateway, inbox, PGlite memory, Electrobun/Bun app.
- **Builder confirmation:** verified/Premium builder account `@dEXploarer` had the exact CA in bio and a pinned/post launch reference to Detour/site. This is stronger than holder/community claims.
- **Pump/Dex metadata alignment:** Pump.fun metadata and DexScreener linked the same website/X/GitHub context.
- **Microcap entry:** roughly $18k-$36k MC with ~$10k-$14k liquidity and good/locked LP scanner score.

## Cautions

- TwitterAPI Latest/Top may be 402-blocked; use fallbacks (`fxtwitter`, `vxtwitter`, Jina Reader, GitHub API/raw README, website HTML) and explicitly label Latest/Top unavailable.
- A tiny project mascot/account (DTOUR: `@detour_squirrel`, ~27 followers/no visible posts) is not enough alone. Conviction came from the verified builder profile and repo/product evidence.
- Keep risks explicit: experimental product, low liquidity, volatile chart, low-LP-provider warning, and unproven organic traction.

## Verdict guidance

Classify as **APE** when all of the following are true:

1. The product is real/non-generic and independently inspectable (site + repo/app/install/docs).
2. The builder/founder/project owner directly carries the exact CA or posts it from their own account.
3. The chain/security checks are acceptable for degen sizing.
4. Market cap is micro/early enough that the real-product/builder-confirmed edge matters.

If the CA is only on a tiny mascot/project account with no builder/founder confirmation, or the repo/site is superficial/vibecoded, keep it at WATCH or SKIP depending on quality.
